Where our revenue comes from

We operate as a distribution business. This means we do not charge you direct advisory fees. Instead, we are compensated through commissions paid by asset management companies for the products (MF, AIF, PMS, SIF) we distribute.

This model allows us to offer our implementation services, behavioral coaching, and research-backed portfolio construction without you having to write a separate check.
